Are you a software designer? You're building applications by the power of UML? Or are you a software engineer in an agile team transforming user stories into tests to be put in your TDD process? Are you a product manager defining new services in your service oriented architecture and need someone to melt down your specification into sourcecode? Put up your requirements, specifications, designs and tests here and let the crowd of supercool programmers out there get their hands on it. Show them your appreciation and pay them when they prove their expertise.

Do you love programming? You're maybe coding all day long at work and still find your seat at home in front of the screen to spend an hour or two in the evening coding for fun (and what tasks you picked for yourself, but been given by your manager). FAQ

What is a designer?
A designer is someone who builds the big picture of a piece of software. He overviews the interactions of all the modules that for mthe software, all the services that play together. He can tell exactly what a new piece of software has to perform to perfectly fit into the architecture.
What is a coder?
A coder is a specialist of the language in use. Equipped with his favorite IDE and given the exact specifications and requirements of the software he is able to produce the most elegant and performant code that fits the demand of the service.
